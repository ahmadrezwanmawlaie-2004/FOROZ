About Us

FOROZ is a non-profit organization established on September 6, 2025. This organization provides a foundation of education and opportunites for youth to develop their potential talents.

Preparing Students to Achieve Success

Developing Confident and

Successful Learners

By providing online educational courses in various subjects, we can help all youths and students, particularly those who are disadvantaged in schools, universities, and educational programs, to unlock and nurture their potential talents. This organization offers a variety of online courses in subjects such as English, Mathematics, computer skills, and many others, all with high-quality standards and materials.
